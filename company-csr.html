BeeHyv is privileged to be associated with some outstanding NGO's who are making an enormous difference in the lives of children.

Akshaya Patra Foundation
Akshaya Patra provides nutritious mid-day meals to around 10 million under-privileged children a day as part of the mid-day meal school program, thus reducing drop-out rates in school. This initiative is a great example of involvement of NGO's and private sector firms along with the government to bring about a positive change in society. It has got accolades from none less than the distinguished former President of India, Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam and President Barack Obama of the US.

BeeHyv makes yearly contributions to Akshaya Patra since 2009.

"We thank BeeHyv for its regular contributions from 2009 onwards till now towards the cause of Akshaya Patra. We greatly thank you for your donations over the years to our foundation to make children attend the schools and get the education. With this support, so far we have fed about 2100 children, a free lunch for a full year, so that they don't drop out of school for want of food and continue education. It was a great gesture from BeeHyv to have selected such a noble cause as to not to allow a child to drop out of education because of hunger. We thank your organization once again for your increasing support every year. "

Satya Gaura Chandra Dasa
President - Akshaya Patra Foundation (Hyderabad)

Chehak Trust
BeeHyv made a contribution to the Sahyog initiative of the Chehak Trust(sahyogchehak.org).

Sahyog is a community based education and health initiative of the CHEHAK TRUST(reg no. E - 21112), which is registered under the Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950. Sahyog works in two slum areas of Mumbai, Jari Mari Kurla and Dindoshi, Goregaon. Sahyog's various activities are largely directed at improving the situation of healthcare and education in the community. Without attempting to duplicate existing efforts - both in the government and voluntary sector, Sahyog aims to improve access to these services both by collaborating with existing organisations as well as increasing community awareness and mobilisation. The long-term objective of Sahyog is to create a secular, progressive and empowering space, which enables the development of a cadre of well-trained, socially committed young leaders.

BeeHyv's contribution was utilised for renovation work(like building a toilet and replacing a ladder) in schools and other activities.
