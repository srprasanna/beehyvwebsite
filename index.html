Who are we?

We are a software product engineering firm based in India providing Outsourced Software Product Development (OPD) services. We are experts in developing products on multiple technology platforms. We offer niche, high end but cost effective skills in developing world class products. Majority of our engineers are alumni of Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), a premier institute of technology in the world.
