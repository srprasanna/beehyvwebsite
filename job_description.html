Job Description for Infrastructure Support Engineer


Job Title:- Infrastructure Support Engineer
Company Name :- Beehyv Software Solutions
Location :- Delhi
Experience :- 2 -3 years of experience

Job Summary:-
This requirement is for a prestigious pan India healthcare initiative being funded by a very reputed global foundation working together with the Government of India. The responsibilities include
  • Maintaining and deploying application/reporting production servers, database servers and Nagios servers.
  • Monitoring servers, logs and database backups.
  • Debugging, troubleshooting and real time fix for Production issues.
The engineer should be willing to work in the following shifts:
  • 6 AM – 3 PM
  • 1 PM – 10 PM

Job Requirements:-
  • 1) Must have experience in installing, configuring and monitoring Nagios.
  • 2) Must have experience in configuring and debugging Apache and Apache Tomcat.
  • 3) Must have experience in configuring and managing firewalls.
  • 4) Good to have experience in supporting Production Servers.
  • 5) Should have good command over basic desktop as well server networking (like DNS Configuration, DHCP awareness, Load Balancing etc.)
  • 6) Must have experience in Deploying Web Projects with Hudson and Apache Tomcat.
  • 7) Must have experience in configuring SSL Certificates for Production Servers.
  • 8) Good to have experience in basic Linux scripting and commands.
  • 9) Mandatory experience in installation and restoring differential backups of My Sql and PostGres Sql.
