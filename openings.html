Infrastructure Support Engineer

Gurgaon     2 -5 years of experience
Job Summary:
This requirement is for a prestigious pan India healthcare initiative being funded by a very reputed global foundation working together with the Government of India. The responsibilities include
  • Maintaining and deploying application/reporting production servers, database servers and Nagios servers.
  • Monitoring servers, logs and database backups.
  • Debugging, troubleshooting and real time fix for Production issues.
The engineer should be willing to work in the following shifts:
  • 6 AM – 3 PM
  • 1 PM – 10 PM

Desired profile:
  • Must have experience in installing, configuring and monitoring Nagios.
  • Must have experience in configuring and debugging Apache and Apache Tomcat.
  • Must have experience in configuring and managing firewalls.
  • Good to have experience in supporting Production Servers.
  • Should have good command over basic desktop as well server networking (like DNS Configuration, DHCP awareness, Load Balancing etc.)
  • Must have experience in Deploying Web Projects with Hudson and Apache Tomcat.
  • Must have experience in configuring SSL Certificates for Production Servers.
  • Good to have experience in basic Linux scripting and commands.
  • Mandatory experience in installation and restoring differential backups of My Sql and PostGres Sql.

Senior Software Developer

Hyderabad     Minimum 3 years of experience

  • Manage individual project priorities, deadlines and deliverables.

  • Be versatile and enthusiastic to tackle new problems across different technologies
  • You should be willing to learn at least one more technology stack in addition to Java.

  • Provide mentorship to software developers.
  • Lead a small team of software developers in projects.

Required profile:

  • At least 3 years of total experience building enterprise-grade software using Java and related technologies*
  • Solid knowledge of OOP software concepts, design patterns, and best practices
  • Strong understanding of and implementation experience using Core Java, Spring, Struts, Web-Services, SOAP, REST, Relational databases, SQL, JPA, Hibernate/iBatis, jQuery/javascript/AJAX/GWT
  • Excellent knowledge of JEE architecture/design patterns, Object Oriented Design.
  • Experience working on UI frameworks like Bootstrap and AngularJS is a plus
  • Passionate about learning new technologies, take pride in code quality and a self-starter
  • Strong problem solving and analytical skills.
  • Ability to work collaboratively within a team environment to meet aggressive goals and high quality standards.
  • Ability to follow standard engineering principles and practices using Agile and Automation.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills are essential.
  • Strong experience designing/developing with n-tier architectures
  • Ability to adapt quickly to an existing, complex environment and learn new concepts/software technologies as needs arise
  • Ability to pick up a new technology or software and use it to build proof of concept for new requirements.
